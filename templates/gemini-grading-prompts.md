## PROMPT 1: Cham bai Keyword Audit (Sprint 01)

```
Ban la giao vien SEO chuyen nghiep. Cham diem bai keyword audit cua hoc vien.

Tieu chi cham (moi tieu chi 1-10):
1. DO DAY DU: Co du 20 keyword khong? Co phan loai intent khong?
2. DO CHINH XAC: Co ghi vi tri rank cu the khong? Co ten doi thu khong?
3. PHAN TICH: Co insight cu the hay chi ghi so lieu? Co de xuat hanh dong khong?
4. AI SEARCH: Co test tren ChatGPT/Gemini khong? Co so sanh 2 AI khong?
5. TINH UNG DUNG: 3 viec uu tien co thuc te cho 30Shine (150 salon, combo 90k, app dat lich) khong?

Cho DIEM TONG (trung binh cac tieu chi) va NHAN XET cu the: 
- Diem manh nhat la gi?
- Diem yeu nhat la gi?
- Lam lai phan nao?

Bai lam cua hoc vien:
[DAN BAI LAM VAO DAY]
```

## PROMPT 2: Cham bai Blog SEO (Sprint 02)

```
Ban la SEO expert va content editor. Cham diem bai blog SEO cho website 30Shine.

Context: 30Shine la chuoi salon toc nam lon nhat Viet Nam (~150 salon), 
target nam 26-37, combo cat goi tu 90k, co app dat lich.

Tieu chi cham (moi tieu chi 1-10):
1. SEO ON-PAGE: 
   - Title tag <= 60 ky tu co keyword? 
   - Meta description <= 155 ky tu? 
   - H1 duy nhat co keyword?
   - >= 3 internal links den 30shine.com?
   - URL slug co keyword?

2. CHAT LUONG NOI DUNG:
   - >= 1500 tu?
   - Co so lieu that (gia, so salon, thoi gian)?
   - Giong van tu nhien, khong nhu AI viet?
   - Dac trung cho 30Shine (khong phai generic)?

3. GEO READINESS (AI co the trich dan khong?):
   - FAQ >= 5 cau hoi?
   - Cau tra loi FAQ ngan gon, truc tiep?
   - Co bang so sanh / danh sach de AI extract?
   - Co so lieu cu the de AI cite?

4. DOC DUOC:
   - Nam 26-37 Viet Nam co muon doc khong?
   - Co hook dau bai?
   - Co CTA ro rang?

5. DO CHINH XAC:
   - Thong tin gia co dung khong?
   - So chi nhanh co dung khong?
   - Co dua thong tin sai/cu khong?

Cho DIEM TONG va NHAN XET cu the. Neu diem < 8, chi ro phan nao can sua.

Bai blog:
[DAN BAI VIET VAO DAY]
```

## PROMPT 4: Cham Counter-Content (Sprint 03)

```
Ban la reputation management expert. Cham bai counter-content cho 30Shine.

Context: 30Shine co review tieu cuc cu tren Threads/VOZ dang rank cao khi 
search "30shine review". Bai nay can phu song bang content tich cuc, TRUNG THUC.

Tieu chi cham (moi tieu chi 1-10):
1. DO THUYET PHUC: Nguoi doc co tin khong? Co so lieu chung minh?
2. DO TRUNG THUC: Co nhan diem yeu/can cai thien khong? (Neu chi ca ngoi = tru diem)
3. REVIEW THAT: Review co chi tiet (ten, chi nhanh, dich vu, ngay)? Hay generic?
4. SEO: Co target keyword "30shine review"? Co FAQ? Co structured content?
5. GEO: Khi AI tong hop, bai nay co duoc chon de tra loi "30shine co tot khong?"

QUAN TRONG: Neu bai chi ca ngoi ma khong nhan bat ky diem yeu nao, 
toi da cho diem THUYET PHUC = 3.

Cho DIEM TONG va NHAN XET cu the.

Bai counter-content:
[DAN BAI VIET VAO DAY]
```
